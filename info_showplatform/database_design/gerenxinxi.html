About MOX

MOX is music television that actually plays music. Crazy, huh?

Our welcome message to you

As you probably noticed already, MOX is a 24/7 continuous stream of music videos, interviews, studio sessions, and festival coverage. On top of that, every 15 minutes one of our VJs brings you the latest music news from around the world. Tune in, and leave MOX on to stay connected to music all day long. Why has no one done this before? We have no idea.

MOX is currently in beta. Over the next few months, we'll be bringing some of our thousands of hours of original footage online, adding functionality to the site, and incorporating your feedback into our programming schedule so we know what you'd like to see. So please help us find bugs and let us know what you think of MOX clicking on the feedback link at the top of the screen. Thanks!
